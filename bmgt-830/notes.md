## Aug 29 -- Intro

### 3 parts:

- Objective Function: e.g. $max$ of $f(x)$
- Constraints (subject to):
  - $g_1 (x) \le b_1$
  - $g_2 (x) \le b_2$
  - ...
  - $g_n (x) \le b_n$
- Decision Variables: $x$


### Graphic Approach: works for 2 variables

Plotting all the lines and mark the area by the constraints, then apply the decision variable function and figure out the corner points (or could be a line in some case)
